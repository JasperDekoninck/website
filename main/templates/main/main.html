About this site

Welcome to my site I would say! If you've found your way to this site, you probably know what's in it, but in case you do not: this site is simply a platform where I showcase some cool/fun/random projects that I enjoy. You can play several games and compare your highscores with the highscores of your friends. You can take a look at some of my random projects or you can read some mathematical papers I wrote in the last couple of years. I hope you enjoy it! If you've any suggestions for projects, you can always contact me. The projects are currently seperated in the following categories:

Games

I like creating simple games. They are perfect to test your programming skills without having to learn much graphic-related stuff (I suck at those things). Here you can find and play some of the old time classics, so I hope you're going to enjoy them.

Mathematics

Mathematics is another topic I have an enormous interest for. I just put here same random mathematical topics I like. Hope you enjoy 😀

Random

Sometimes I just make some random things I like or something that seems fun to do. All of those projects can be found here. I hope you might find inspiration for projects yourself 😉

About me

My name is Jasper Dekoninck and I'm currently a student at the University of Leuven where I study Mathematics and Physics. However, I've always had a great interest in programming. I first started out in Javascript, but didn't like it that much (because I was a total noob). Then I started programming in Python and that remained my main language for several years. I learned how to create games, do data science and have some fun creating mathematical programs. Recently I've rediscovered Javascript and on this site I combine both Python and Javascript (+ CSS/HTML) to create my biggest project yet. Hope you like it 😀.
